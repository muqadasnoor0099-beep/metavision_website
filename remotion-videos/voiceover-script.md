# HA EngagePro — Voiceover Script
**Video duration:** 70 seconds | **Target voice:** Female, warm & professional | **Pace:** ~130 words/minute

---

## How to generate this voice

Paste the **Full Script** section below into one of these free tools:

| Tool | Best Free Voice | Link |
|---|---|---|
| **ElevenLabs** (recommended) | Rachel / Bella / Elli | elevenlabs.io |
| **Murf.ai** | Natalie / Julia | murf.ai |
| **Play.ht** | Emily / Olivia | play.ht |
| **Azure TTS** | Jenny (Neural) | speech.microsoft.com/portal |

**Settings to use:**
- Style: `conversational` or `narration`
- Speed: `0.95` (slightly slower than default — gives words room to breathe)
- Export as: **MP3 or WAV**, 44.1 kHz

**After generating:**
1. Save the file as `voiceover.mp3`
2. Place it at: `remotion-videos/public/audio/voiceover.mp3`
3. Run `npm run studio` to preview with audio

## Full Script (paste this into the TTS tool — all at once)

Hi, let me introduce HA EngagePro — a management platform built for finance companies, and currently used by HarAik Global.

It streamlines the entire client lifecycle — from onboarding and project initiation, all the way to delivering outputs and managing client requests — all in one place.

Clients can easily submit information, while teams collaborate without relying on scattered tools.

The platform also supports consultations across firms — enabling broader collaboration and keeping every team member focused on exactly what needs to be done next.

For leadership, HA EngagePro offers powerful analytics — from senior-level pipeline insights, down to detailed operational data.

Plus, every user experience is tailored based on their role — ensuring both efficiency and security across the entire organization.

HA EngagePro is VAPT-approved and integrated with industry-standard authentication systems — making it a secure and scalable solution.

Ready to transform your firm's operations? Explore HA EngagePro today.
